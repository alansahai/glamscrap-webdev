Policy

Environmental Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

1. Policy Statement

We are committed to environmental stewardship and sustainable development through innovative waste management and recycling. Our efforts exceed compliance and focus on proactive protection and improvement.

2. Our Environmental Vision and Mission

2.1 Environmental Vision

To be a catalyst for environmental transformation by achieving zero waste to landfill and maximizing resource recovery.

2.2 Environmental Mission

To implement innovative recycling technologies that reduce environmental impact and support a sustainable future.

3. Environmental Objectives and Commitments

3.1 Core Commitments

  • Zero Waste to Landfill
  • Carbon Footprint Reduction
  • Resource Conservation
  • Pollution Prevention
  • Circular Economy
  • Biodiversity Protection

3.2 Quantitative Targets

  • 99.8% e-waste material recovery
  • 95% waste diversion from landfills
  • 10% annual reduction in energy per ton
  • Carbon neutrality by 2030

4. Environmental Management Framework

4.1 Management System

  • ISO 14001:2015 based EMS
  • Continuous monitoring
  • Internal and external audits

4.2 Risk Assessment

  • Air, water, soil risk management
  • Emergency and spill control
  • Hazardous material handling

5. Operational Environmental Standards

5.1 Waste Processing

  • E-Waste: Secure data destruction, safe battery handling
  • Plastics: Advanced sorting, food-grade output
  • Industrial: Waste-to-energy, hazardous compliance

5.2 Facility Operations

  • Energy: LED, renewable integration
  • Water: Rainwater harvesting, recycling
  • Green Building: Sustainable design and materials

6. Compliance and Certifications

  • Adheres to CPCB, TNPCB, and all Indian environmental laws
  • Maintains ISO 14001 and BIS certifications
  • Holds Pollution Control Board and EPR authorizations

7. Stakeholder Engagement

  • Client Education and Sustainability Reporting
  • Community Involvement Programs
  • Industry, Government, and Academic Collaboration

8. Performance Monitoring

  • KPIs: Recovery rates, energy/water use, emissions
  • Real-time air/water monitoring, monthly reviews
  • Annual third-party audits

9. Training and Awareness

  • Staff training on safety and environmental impact
  • Supplier compliance and reporting requirements

10. Innovation and R&D

  • Investment in recycling tech and digital waste tracking
  • Partnerships with universities and startups

11. Emergency Preparedness

  • Plans for chemical spills, natural disasters
  • Business continuity and recovery protocols

12. Transparency and Reporting

  • Annual reports and performance metrics
  • Open stakeholder consultations and data access

13. Continuous Improvement

  • Regular reviews, audits, feedback integration
  • Employee and customer suggestions encouraged

14. Supplier & Partner Standards

  • Environmental compliance and transparency
  • Shared improvement goals

15. Implementation & Governance

  • Environmental Committee and Officers oversee execution
  • Monthly reviews and resource allocation ensured

16. Communication

This policy is communicated to all employees and made available publicly through our website. It is reviewed annually.

17. Management Commitment

Our leadership pledges full support for environmental protection, resource allocation, and continuous improvement.
