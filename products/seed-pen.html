Recycled Seed Pens


Product Description

Manufacturing & Materials: Our plantable seed pens are made from 100% recycled paper tubes combined with natural corn-based bioplastics, filled with soy-based, non-toxic ink cartridges. Each pen features a biodegradable seed capsule embedded in the cap, containing carefully selected seeds like herbs, flowers, or vegetables with good germination rates. The recycled paper is tightly rolled and treated with eco-friendly, water-resistant coatings that safely break down in soil after use.

Features & Benefits: Perfect for students, professionals, and eco-conscious users, these plantable pens provide smooth writing performance before transforming into useful plants. Available with seed options like chillies, basil, and marigolds. The pens can be customized with eco-friendly printing for branding, corporate gifting, or sustainability programs. Designed with a comfortable grip and smooth-flowing ink for everyday writing tasks, these pens help reduce waste by converting discarded writing instruments into plants, supporting green living.

Environmental Impact

Plastic Waste Reduction

Each pen prevents approximately 5-7 conventional plastic pens from entering landfills by providing a completely biodegradable alternative that decomposes naturally.

Carbon Footprint

Reduces CO2 emissions by 55% compared to plastic pens through biodegradable materials and carbon-negative end-of-life as growing plants absorb atmospheric carbon.

Resource Conservation

Uses 60% less petroleum-based materials and 40% less energy in production while creating plants that contribute to air purification and oxygen production.

Circular Economy

Creates a regenerative cycle where office waste becomes productive plants, supporting urban gardening, food security, and workplace wellness programs.

Lifetime Impact

Each pen can grow into plants that produce food, medicine, or pollinator habitat while eliminating plastic pen waste and contributing to biodiversity restoration.
