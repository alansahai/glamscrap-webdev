Our Products

Sustainable By-products

High-quality materials created through our advanced recycling processes.

Recycled Pens with Seed

Revolutionary plantable pens made from biodegradable materials that write smoothly during use, then transform into flourishing plants when planted in soil after the ink runs out. Each eco-friendly pen contains organic seeds in the cap and prevents 5–7 plastic pens from entering landfills while contributing to urban gardening and carbon reduction.

Recycled Pencils with Seed

Innovative plantable pencils made from sustainable cedar wood with embedded seed capsules that grow into herbs, flowers, or vegetables when planted after use. Each eco-friendly pencil provides smooth #2 graphite writing performance while eliminating pencil waste entirely, transforming discarded stubs into thriving plants that contribute to biodiversity and food production.

Recycled Notepads

Premium spiral notepads crafted from 100% post-consumer recycled paper, transforming 200–300 sheets of discarded office papers and cardboard into high-quality writing surfaces. Each eco-friendly notepad reduces CO₂ emissions by 65% compared to virgin paper products while saving approximately 0.5 trees and replacing up to 500 loose sheets throughout its lifetime.

Momentos made from Recycled Materials

Transform waste into prestigious awards with our customized trophies made from 100% recycled plastics, turning discarded bottles and containers into beautiful recognition pieces. Each trophy prevents 15–25 plastic items from polluting landfills while delivering superior durability and stunning personalized designs that celebrate achievement and environmental responsibility.

Recycled Bags

Durable reusable bags crafted from 50–75 discarded plastic shopping bags, transforming waste into strong, water-resistant carriers with reinforced stitching and comfortable handles. Each eco-friendly bag reduces CO₂ emissions by 80% compared to virgin plastic production while replacing up to 1,000 single-use plastic bags throughout its lifetime.
